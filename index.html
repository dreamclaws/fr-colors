Welcome to my comprehensive color guide! I originally created this because I wanted a way to see all genes for a color at a glance when scrying G1s (so I could find potential matching/accent colors more easily), and figured it would be useful to other people as well.

Please message me on FR or Discord (tigerfeather#8582) if you find any errors or have any questions.
